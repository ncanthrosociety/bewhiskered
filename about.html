Bewhiskered

Bewhiskered is a new furry convention run by the NC Anthro Society in Durham, North Carolina. Our 2020 dates are Friday, October 02 - Sunday, October 04. We will be located in the lovely Marriott at Research Triangle Park, just a short drive away from Raleigh and RDU Airport.

Convention Theme: Blast Off!

Our inaugural theme is “Blast Off,” both for the convention’s official debut, and in honor of the 50th anniversary of Apollo 13. Join us as we hearken back to the retro futurism of the Apollo era and celebrate the start of an epic journey in the only way we know how: a three day weekend full of friends, entertainment, and much much more.

NC Anthro Society

NCAS is a nonprofit anthropomorphic organization operating out of Raleigh, North Carolina. They wish to give back to the furry community by putting on great events, and bringing groups together in cooperation.
